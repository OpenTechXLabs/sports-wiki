Kabaddi Sports

Sport Overview
Hockey is a fast-paced team sport played on ice or field. It involves two teams using sticks to hit a puck or ball into the opponent's goal to score points.

Rules and Regulations
Hockey has specific rules governing gameplay, player conduct, and scoring. Understanding these rules is crucial for fair and competitive play.

Equipment and Gear
Hockey players wear protective gear, including helmets, pads, and skates. They use sticks to control the puck or ball during the game.

Playing Field/Court
Hockey can be played on ice or field, depending on the variant. The playing area is marked with goals and zones for gameplay.

Famous Players
Hockey has produced many legendary players known for their skills and contributions to the sport. Some of the greatest hockey players in history include Wayne Gretzky, Bobby Orr, Mario Lemieux, and Sidney Crosby.
